# Colonial Government
## Colonial Explorer

_Power armour equipped explorers on the far frontier._

| Firepower | Mobility | Assault | Discipline | Scale | Points |
| --------- | -------- | ------- | ---------- | ----- | ------ |
| D6        | D12      | D10     | D8         | 2     | 36     |

### Jump Packs
Active: Use prior to moving the unit. Add 5" to the maximum move distance for that move. Additionally this move ignores terrain, any tokens, and other units.

### Flame Units
Active: Use at any point while within 5" of an enemy unit, that unit immediately becomes **shaken**.

## Stalker

_Government assassins equipped with black market anti-personnel weapons._

| Firepower | Mobility | Assault | Discipline | Scale | Points |
| --------- | -------- | ------- | ---------- | ----- | ------ |
| D12       | D8       | D8      | D10        | 1     | 38     |

### Knife Missile
Active: As a **strategic action** place a token within 5" of this unit. As a **strategic action** you may move this token up to 5" ignoring terrain, other models and tokens. If at any point this token is within 5" of an enemy unit, that unit becomes **shaken** and the token is removed.

### AG Harness
Passive: For the purposes of movement, this unit counts terrain of scale 3 or less as scale 0.

## Patrol Drone

_Cheap and reliable tech from the dawn of the 21st century._

| Firepower | Mobility | Assault | Discipline | Scale | Points |
| --------- | -------- | ------- | ---------- | ----- | ------ |
| D4        | D10      | D4      | D4         | 1     | 22     |
### Flying
Passive: This unit ignores terrain, any tokens, and other units when it moves.

### Self Destruct
Active: As a **strategic action** you may detonate the drone. All units (including friendly units) within 5" become **shaken**. Remove the drone from the battlefield.
