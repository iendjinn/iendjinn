Serious Injuries and Phobias
# Tests
  • To make a test roll 1D20 + attribute to equal or exceed DR.
  • Take 1 stress before rolling for +4 on a test.
  • Add 4 if you have a relevant specialism.
  • A natural 20 is a critical.
  • If you roll equal or under stress you panic.
# Combat
  • Aimed Fire: Test dexterity.
  • Auto Fire: Test strength.
  • Hip Fire: Test agility.
  • Melee: Test strength.
  • Take Cover/Dodge: Test agility.
  • Block: Test strength.
# Armour and Wounds

Expend an armour point to ignore a wound, or 2 points per wound for armour piercing attacks. If you reach or exceed your max wounds, roll on the critical injury table.

# Supplies and Ammo

When you use a consumable, test supply. On a 1-2 the supply rating decreases. Supply ratings: D20, D12, D10, D8, D6, D4.

Test ammo supply after combat, supplying rating decreases on 1-4 if auto-fire was used.

# Rest and Recovery

Relax in a safe place for 15 minutes to remove 1 stress.

If you can sleep:

  • In shifts, keeping watch: Remove 1 wound
  • A full eight hours: Remove 1 wound and 1 stress
